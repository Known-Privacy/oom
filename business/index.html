Protect your business.

Phishing and "Smishing" attacks have gotten more refined. The Opt-Out Machine can help you minimize the danger to your business.

Address the #1 security risk

Phishing is a type of social engineering where an attacker sends a fraudulent (e.g., spoofed, fake, or otherwise deceptive) message designed to trick a person into revealing sensitive information to the attacker.

Employees are increasingly targeted by using personal data that is available on the internet. The more data an attacker has about your employees, the more easily they may trick someone in your company.

Give executives their focus back

Every interruption switches your employees focus from their jobs to an outbound sales person or marketer. Take the data away from aggressive marketers, sales people and recruiters so they won't be able to create those distractions so easily.

Be proactive

Personal data about your executives and employees is everywhere. But thanks to privacy laws, you can take action to reduce your company's data footprint. And, your employees will thank you.
